Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Gambling Blocker is a Chrome extension that helps you avoid gambling websites by showing a warning popup when you visit them.

Data we collect

We collect nothing. Gambling Blocker does not transmit any data to any server, anywhere.

Data stored on your device

Your settings (which sites are blocked or unblocked, and any custom sites you add) are stored locally using Chrome's built-in chrome.storage.sync API. This means your settings sync across your own Chrome devices if you are signed into Chrome — but they are never sent to us or any third party.

Browsing activity

The extension checks the URL of every page you visit to determine whether to show the warning popup. This check happens entirely within your browser. No URLs, hostnames, or browsing history are recorded or transmitted.

Third-party services

None. The extension makes no network requests of its own.

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published at this URL.
